Safety First

Safety Guidelines

  • Always meet in public places within campus initially
  • Never share sensitive personal information
  • Report suspicious behavior immediately
  • Trust your instincts - if something feels off, it probably is
  • Keep your matches within the Somaiya community

Our Safety Measures

  • Email Verification: Only verified Somaiya email addresses are allowed to register
  • Profile Moderation: All profiles are reviewed before going live
